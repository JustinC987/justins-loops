About Justin's Loops

Hey. Welcome to this blog. The names Justin and I play guitar quite annoyingly. I pretty much made this site to practice Angular but also wanted to have a little fun programming. I will be posting two kinds of content on this site, blog posts with some jams I created, and album discussions.

You see, I got a thing for loops and delay and usually spend hours creating some pretty cool atmospheric tracks that I'd love to share here. These tracks are all live and have zero production. Just me messing around with my pedals.

Home Page

On the home page, you will find posts consisting of embedded (Yes I am lazy) Youtube videos of my bops. From there I may comment on the song I created and possibly even add some artwork that I feel fits the song. There is an archive that contains all of the blog posts as well. Feel free to contact me for whatever odd reason by clicking the contact link in the header. From there a form will display and I think you can figure out the rest.

My Music Page

The second kind of posts I will be making on this site are found in the MyMusic link (guess where that is located?) These posts will consist of me posting an album I really like and me just geeking out over the music I love. Eventually I may add more user interaction like comments but that is up to father time.

I apologize in advance for either putting you to sleep or giving you a headache with my loops.
